```
prepick {card}

Allows a user to prepick a card during the draft provided
the draft has fired and the card has not been picked before.

    example: !prepick lightning bolt
    - The drafter prepicks the card lightning bolt.
    
    alternate command names for prepick:

    - 'Prepick'
    - 'Pre_pick'
    - 'pre_pick'
    - 'Pp'
    - 'pp'```
